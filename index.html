Student Chapter of Computer Society of India

DJCSI represents the Computer Society of India at the college level and is affiliated with Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering, It is the official student chapter of the Department of Information Technology.It is the oldest student chapter of DJ Sanghvi College of Engineering. It organises many events like Codeshastra hackathons, Pitch Please and many more.
